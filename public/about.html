Motto

Walk in the Spirit.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deeply impact generations with the love and gospel of Jesus Christ, guided by the Holy Spirit. We achieve this through fervent prayer, impactful preaching, comprehensive teaching, empowering support, liberating deliverance, and meaningful contributions to social development. Additionally, we strive to be a prophetic voice to the nations, promoting truth, righteousness, and justice.

Our Vision

Empowering lives through a transformative journey of intimacy with Christ and the dynamic proclamation of the good news.

Core Values

  1. Truth:

    Pursuit and adherence to divine truth as revealed in scriptures.

  2. Holiness and Purity:

    • Holiness: Encouragement for a life dedicated to spiritual purity and moral uprightness.

  3. Spiritual Virtues:

    • Love: Promotion of compassion, kindness, and benevolence.
    • Excellence: Encouragement to pursue high standards in all endeavors.
    • Faith: Advocacy for a strong belief in God and reliance on divine guidance.

  4. Moral and Ethical Principles:

    • Goodness: Promotion of inherently good and righteous actions.
    • Self-control: Encouragement of discipline over thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.

  5. Spiritual Disciplines:

    • Prayer and Fasting: Highlighting the significance of regular communication with God and spiritual discipline.

  6. Scriptural Emphasis:

    • Word: Stressing the importance of studying, understanding, and living by the teachings of the Bible.

  7. Community and Fellowship:

    • Fellowship: Emphasis on a supportive and connected church community.
    • Praise and Worship: Encouragement for vibrant expressions of gratitude and reverence through worship.

  1. Consistency and Integrity:

    • Consistency: Advocacy for steadfastness and reliability in character and actions.
    • Integrity: Promotion of honesty, transparency, and a strong moral code.

  2. Attitude of Gratitude:

    • Thankfulness: Fostering an attitude of gratitude for God's blessings and provisions.

  3. Generosity:

    • Cheerful Giving: Encouraging a joyful and generous spirit in contributing to the needs of the church and community.

  4. Servant Leadership:

    • Servant Leadership: Emphasizes leading with humility, prioritizing the needs of others, and fostering a culture of service.

  5. Commitment in Service to God:

    • Commitment in Service: Encourages unwavering dedication and loyalty in serving God, acknowledging the responsibilities that come with this commitment.

  6. Unity of Purpose in Ministry and Strong Family Values:

    • Acknowledging the importance of unity in purpose within ministry and fostering strong family values.
