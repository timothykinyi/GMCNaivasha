Welcome to Global Methodist Church - Naivasha

At Global Methodist Church - Naivasha, we are more than just a congregation; we are a family united by the love and grace of Jesus Christ. As you explore our website, we hope you will feel the warmth and hospitality that defines our church community.

Our church is a place where you can find refuge, renewal, and a sense of belonging. We are dedicated to creating an environment that fosters spiritual growth, genuine fellowship, and heartfelt worship. Here, you will find open hearts and open doors, ready to welcome you into our church family.

We believe that every person is a cherished creation of God, deserving of love, acceptance, and compassion. Whether you are on a journey to deepen your faith, seeking answers to life's big questions, or simply looking for a community to call home, you will find a place here at Global Methodist Church - Naivasha.

Join us for worship, connect with others through our various ministries, and experience the transforming power of God's love. No matter where you are in your spiritual journey, you are welcome here.
