Help and FAQs

If you have questions or need assistance, please check our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) below:

  • When are your church services held?

    Our church services are held every Sunday morning from 10:00 AM to 12:30pm.

  • Do you have programs for children and youth?

    Yes, we have vibrant ministries for children and youth of all ages. Our Children's Ministry offers age-appropriate activities and teachings during Sunday services, while our Youth Ministry provides opportunities for teens to connect, grow, and serve.

  • How can I get involved in serving at the church?

    We encourage everyone to get involved in serving! Whether you're interested in music, hospitality, outreach, or teaching, there are numerous opportunities to serve. Feel free to reach out to our church office or speak with one of our pastors to learn more about available serving opportunities.

  • Is there a small group or Bible study I can join?

    Yes, we offer various small groups and Bible studies throughout the week. These groups provide opportunities for deeper fellowship, study, and prayer. You can find more information about our small groups and Bible studies by reach out to our church office.

  • How can I give financially to support the church's ministry?

    Thank you for your generosity! We offer several convenient ways to give, including giving via mobile app, and in-person giving during services. Your financial support enables us to continue fulfilling our mission and serving our community.

    For Mpesa and other mobile money transfer services use 400222 as the business number and 34415# followed by the purpose of giving as the account number

  • How can I stay updated on church events and announcements?

    You can stay connected with us by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, or checking our website regularly for updates on upcoming events, services, and announcements.
