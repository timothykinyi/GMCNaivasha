Praise and Worship

Experience the joy of praising God through music and prayer with our Praise and Worship ministry. Join us as we gather to glorify His name, seek His presence, and deepen our spiritual connection. Through dynamic worship sessions and heartfelt expressions of faith, we aim to uplift souls, strengthen community bonds, and draw closer to the heart of God. All are invited to join us on this inspiring journey of worship and spiritual renewal.

Men's Ministry

Discover the power of brotherhood and personal growth in our Men's Ministry. Here, men of all ages gather to deepen their relationship with God, support one another in faith, and embark on a journey of spiritual growth. Through Bible studies, mentorship, and fellowship, we strive to strengthen our connection to Christ, our families, and our community. Join us as we challenge each other to live out our faith boldly and become the men God has called us to be.

Women's Ministry

Welcome to our Women's Ministry—a nurturing space designed for personal growth, spiritual exploration, and community connection. Here, women gather to deepen their relationship with God, support one another through life's challenges, and discover their unique gifts and callings. Through inspiring teachings, heartfelt conversations, and opportunities for service, we're committed to equipping women to thrive in every aspect of their lives. Join us as we journey together, fostering faith, friendship, and transformation in the heart of our church community.

Youth Ministry

Welcome to our Youth Ministry, where faith meets fun, friendship, and growth! We're not your average crew—we're a community of young people on fire for faith, fun, and making a difference. From epic events to heart-to-heart chats, we're here to help you explore your faith, discover who you are, and make a positive impact in the world. Whether you're seeking spiritual guidance, looking to connect with peers, or eager to serve others, you'll find a home here. So, what are you waiting for? Grab your squad and join the adventure as we dive into faith, friendship, and all the good stuff life has to offer! #Kujinice Na Christ

Children Ministry

Step into a world of wonder and faith-building adventures with our Children's Ministry, inspired by the words of Jesus: 'Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.' (Matthew 19:14). Here, children are cherished, nurtured, and encouraged to grow in their relationship with God. Through engaging activities, heartfelt teachings, and loving mentorship, we create a vibrant space where young hearts can flourish spiritually, emotionally, and socially. Join us as we embark on this journey of discovery, faith, and joyful transformation, inviting every child to experience the love and grace of Jesus Christ.

Media Team

Join the movement with our Media Team, where innovation meets ministry! We're a dynamic group of creatives, tech enthusiasts, and storytellers on a mission to share the love of God through cutting-edge media. From captivating visuals to engaging social content, we're dedicated to amplifying the message of hope and inspiration. Whether you're a pro behind the camera, a social media guru, or have a knack for design, there's a spot for you to shine. Come be part of our team as we revolutionize the way we connect, engage, and spread God's love in today's digital world.

More

Ready to explore? Our church is bustling with diverse ministries, each offering unique ways to grow and serve. Check out our Outreach Ministry, where we extend a helping hand to those in need, our Prayer Ministry, where we intercede for our congregation and community, our Small Groups, where deep friendships and spiritual growth flourish, and our Missions Team, where we impact lives globally. And that's just the beginning! With many more ministries tailored to various interests and needs, there's something for everyone to be a part of. Come join us as we journey together in faith and service.
